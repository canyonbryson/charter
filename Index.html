About Us

Our Mission



To deeply engage with Utah charter schools as a strategic partner committed to learning proficiency for every enrolled student. Our collaborative support for effective leadership and efficient charter school operations is animated by a passion for cultivating learning systems grounded in research and shown to increase learning proficiency in K-12 students.


Our Vision



Every student enrolled in a CLP affiliated Utah charter school will obtain the knowledge, skills and dispositions needed to become a confident and contributing adult in their chosen communities.


Our Purpose



Our purpose is to be an opportunity accelerator for Utah’s children as they cultivate a portfolio of learning-assets foundational to the contributions they make to their communities as life-long learners. To fulfill this purpose, we will work as a strategic catalyst for Utah Charter Schools who share our commitment to continuous and sustainable improvement in student learning outcomes for all students
